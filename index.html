This is Happy Meter.

Employee Satisfaction Info
At your fingertips.

Happy meter determines how confident employees are in their success by asking 2 simple questions.

1 - How confident are you in your success at this company?

Your employees are the driving force behind your company, and a confident employee is your most important asset.

2 - How confident are you that this company will be successful?

Have your finger on the pulse of the overall opinion of your company at large. Compare these metrics over time for real-time business analytics.

3 - Profit!!

Okay, so you still have to actually work. But Happy Meter will bring you one step closer to answering that all important question... How are we doing?
